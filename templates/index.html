YOLO-DurianLeaf - Smart Agriculture Powered by AI

DurianLeafGuard is a disease detection model for durian leaves, developed using the powerful YOLOv11 architecture. It is capable of quickly and accurately identifying leaves affected by leaf blight—one of the most common and damaging diseases in durian plants—while also clearly distinguishing them from healthy leaves. With high precision and real-time processing speed, DurianLeafGuard serves as a powerful tool for farmers to monitor plant health instantly, reduce losses, and optimize productivity.

Supports

Helps farmers detect leaf blight on durian early for timely treatment

Drone

Can be integrated into drones for automatic aerial garden monitoring

Robot

Applicable to garden-patrolling robots to inspect leaves and detect diseases while moving

Yield improvement
